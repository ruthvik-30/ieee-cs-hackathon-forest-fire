CO2 Detection

Em500 Co2 sensors are deployed across the forest to detect rising levels of carbondioxide and the temperature of the surrounding enivronment is accounted aswell, drones are deployed if fire is detected.

Fire Detection using Drone

Image processing using opencv is done to detect fire and as drones fly around the CO2 which raised an alarm.

Smoke detection using satellite and fire spread direction prediction

Satellites images of the fire is taken to process to predict the spread of fire.

Animal proximity to fire detection

Animals distance to fire is found out using opencv to the fire and the forest department is notified to evacuate it.
